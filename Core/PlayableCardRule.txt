A led trump card is renegable if your card is the Ace of Hearts, Ace of Trumps, Jack of Trumps or 5 of trumps and the led trumps is of a lower value than your card.

led card is trump
if player has no trumps, all cards are playable
if player has 1 or more trump(s) that can renege the led trump, all cards are playable
if player has 1 or more trump(s) that cannot renege the led trump, only trumps are playable
if player has 1 or more trump(s) that cannot renege the led trump & 1 or more trump(s) that can renege the led trump, only trumps are playable

led card is not a trump
if player has a card with the suit matching the led card, only that suit and trumps are playable
if player has non trumps that do not match the led card suit, all cards are playable
if player has trumps and cards that do not match the led card suit, all cards are playable
