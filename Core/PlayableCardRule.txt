Your trump card can renege the led trump if your card is of a higher value than the led trump and if your card is one of following:

Ace of Hearts,
Ace of Trumps,
Jack of Trumps,
5 of Trumps.

if led card is trump
	if player has no trumps, all cards are playable
	if player has any trumps and they can all renege the led trump, all cards are playable
	if player has atleast 1 trump that cannot renege the led trump, only trumps are playable

	if led card is not a trump
	if player has atleast 1 card with the suit matching the led card, only cards with that suit and trumps are playable
	if player has cards that all do not match the led card suit, all cards are playable
